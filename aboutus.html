About Us

Hunt-Yo-Recipe is a Recipe generator app. This app helps you find meals for the ingredients you already have.

Hunt-Yo-Recipe app uses MealDB API to search the meals using the ingredients of your search. After selecting the meal. You are given more details about the recipe and a detailed youtube video link is present for you to follow through.
